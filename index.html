Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Travelmedz kit?

The Travelmedz kit provides essential medications and supplies for common travel-related health concerns, such as gastrointestinal issues, upper respiratory infections, minor wounds, and pain relief. The kit is airport-ready, and does not contain any controlled substances. The quantity of medicine is suitable for short-term travel; consult your doctor if you are going to be abroad for extended periods of time.

Can I use the prescription medication in the kit at any time?

No, prescription medicines like antibiotics must only be used after a consultation with a TravelMedz healthcare provider. While you can confidently use the over-the-counter items for minor ailments, it is essential to consult our medical team before making use of certain supplies and equipment in the kit. This includes any prescription medication and antibiotics. This is important as a specialist must first ensure that it is the correct and safe treatment for your symptoms.

Who are the doctors I will be speaking with?

Our telehealth service connects you with professionally accredited healthcare providers, all of whom are registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA).

Is the kit a replacement for travel insurance or an emergency service?

No. TravelMedz is not a comprehensive emergency medical service. The kit is designed to handle common, non-emergency health issues and is not a substitute for full medical care. Our doctors may refer you to a health facility, clinic or specialist in your destination country, should they deem it clinically necessary.

How does the telehealth consultation work?

Consultations are conducted exclusively via telehealth, and you will be responsible for providing a stable internet connection and a suitable device. Users must accept the limitations of telehealth, which includes the inability of our doctors to perform a physical examination. Our service is delivered through a third-party platform, and while we strive for consistent access, we are not liable for disruptions caused by the provider.

Can I share the kit with my family or travel companions?

No. The kit is for your personal use only and must not be shared, resold, or distributed. The medical advice provided is tailored to your individual health profile, which you provide upon registration.

How is my personal information handled?

Your privacy is important to us. In compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), your personal information will only be shared with relevant internal personnel and our telehealth consultants for your treatment. By using the service, you consent to your information being used for treatment purposes and service improvement.
