What is Travelmedz?

Travelmedz is a convenient travel medicine kit, paired with access to qualified doctors via an online telehealth service - wherever you are. With an expertly curated selection of trusted medications and round-the-clock virtual medical support, Travelmedz empowers you to manage common health issues swiftly and safely while travelling.

Why Choose Travelmedz?

💊

Immediate access to medication

Our compact, elegantly designed medicine bag includes essentials to treat a range of common travel-related conditions — from pain and fevers to allergies, tummy troubles, respiratory issues, minor wounds, and more.

👨‍⚕️

Talk to a doctor, anytime

Travelmedz provides you access to South African registered healthcare professionals via secure telehealth consultations. Whether you're feeling unwell or unsure which medication to use, help is just a call away.

👤

Personalised care

When you sign up, you provide basic information about yourself, your trip and your health. This means our doctors can offer more tailored, informed advice - no repeating your medical history every time you need help.

✅

Trusted contents, professionally curated

Every item in the Travelmedz kit has been carefully selected by specialist medical professionals to cover the most common health conditions travellers face.

What's Inside the Travelmedz Kit?

Each kit includes a range of over-the-counter and prescription medications and supplies, including:

Pain & inflammation:

Panado, Voltaren GT 50, Aspirin

Cold & allergies:

Iliadin nasal spray, Loratidine, Cepacol cough discs, Sudafed

Tummy troubles:

Azithromycin 500mg, Buscopan, Zofran (anti-nausea), Duphalac, Imodium, Orange Rehidrat

Allergic reactions & skin irritations:

Prednisone 5mg, Anthisan cream, Burnshield, Savlon

Eye care:

Eye Gene drops, Chloramax ointment

Minor injuries:

Dermaplast Aqua plasters, gauze, crepe bandage, transpore tape

Urinary issues:

Urispas, Urizone

Note: Antibiotics and certain medicines must only be used following consultation with a Travelmedz doctor.
